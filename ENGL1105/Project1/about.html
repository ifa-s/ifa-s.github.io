Cover Analysis of The Nine Thousandth Pair of Eyes

The Nine Thousandth Pair of Eyes is an indie rock/post-punk album by Muzzy Mum (Chinese: 麻茲媽, Pinyin: mázīmā), released on June 25th, 2024, under the label Maybe Mars.

This website analyzes and delves deeper into the visual elements and rhetoric used in the cover of the album. It also intends to provide the necessary context needed to understand the references made in the album and the imagery, by analyzing the political and geographical background of the album, as well as the general background of the band, the music label, and overall history of the unique indie rock in early-2000s China. The overall question it serves to answer is "How does this cover’s purpose differ from others (both in the genre and as a whole), and is it effective?"
