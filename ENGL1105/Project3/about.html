Mobile Suit Gundam: Hathaway Movie Analysis

Mobile Suit Gundam: Hathaway is a 2021 film in the Gundam franchise, the first in a trilogy of movies, the rest of which are unreleased. It’s based on the 1989 novel, Mobile Suit Gundam: Hathaway’s Flash . In this website, I will be analyzing how this movie connects with me, as I consider it my favorite movie despite how the movie is decent at best.

Each page of this goes into a different reason, with the main focus being on Gundam as franchise overall, with Gunpla supporting it, and how the movie displays the highlights of the franchise in it's highest quality yet.
