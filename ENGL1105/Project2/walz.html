Social Media Analysis of Tim Walz

Tim Walz is the vice presidential candidate for the Democratic Party in the 2024 presidential election, under Democrat candidate Kamala Harris.

Tim Walz was born in Nebraska in 1964, where he grew up on a farm in a rural area, a key aspect of his relatable, small-town American personality. Later on, he enrolled in the National Guard in Texas, worked as a teacher in China and the United States, and moved to Minnesota where he later became governor (Grant, 2024). The important thing to take away from this is that Walz is not a politician. He wasn’t raised in a rich family and didn’t grow up in a large town or city. He went to a small college, he was a soldier and he was a teacher. These are all essential aspects of his current persona and campaign, painting him as an average small-town American who has worked as a public servant his entire life, making him a relatable yet upstanding citizen for rural Americans all over the country (Sonnevend, 2024), something the Democratic party has historically struggled with. In his political career, Walz has generally supported progressive policies, a point somewhat contradictory to the average rural American. This is part of what makes him so appealing to younger rural voters, as he is a relatable figure (unlike career politicians), but still supports the progressive policies generally favored by young people today (Bennett, 2024).
